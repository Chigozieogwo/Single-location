Reasons Why You Should Visit Nigeria

Sightseeing and Tourist Sites

The city of Benin has numerous tourists attractions and sightseeing places you can enjoy alone or with friends and families. From beautiful rivers to the national parks full of wildlife and the edging palm tree borders, Benin is a city you should visit today.

Cuisine

Benin is known for having the most popular local dishes in Nigeria and Africa as a whole. Their cuisines are known for their simplicity and savoury nature. From pepper rice to banga soup, starch, peanut soup and many others, a visit to Benin will give you an everlasting fulfilment. No pun intended!

Arts and Culture

Benin has one of the most interesting arts and cultural heritage in Africa. Many of their works of art are found in different museums across Europe and America. The city is known to play a significant role in the development of the infamous African slave trade.

Reasons Why You Should Visit Nigeria

what you need to know about Benin

Benin city is the capital of Edo State, Nigeria and is the biggest city in the state. The city is situated approximately 40 kilometers north of the famous Benin River and is the centre of Nigeria’s rubber industry. The indigenous people of the city are Edo, and they speak the Edo language. The people are known for their dress culture, which is among the richest dress cultures in Africa. The population of people living in Benin city is 1,495,800, and this number keeps increasing. The city’s glorious sightseeing places, tourist attractions, museums, magnificent cultural architecture and unforgettable markets are among the reasons why you should visit Benin city.
